How to Use

Add this simple code to your website before the closing </body> tag:

<!-- Chatbot Widget --> <script src="chatbot.js"></script> <script> ChatbotWidget.init({ botId: 'demo-bot-123', primaryColor: '#4A90E2', position: 'bottom-right' }); </script>

That's it! Your chatbot is now live on your website. Click the chat icon in the bottom right corner to try it! ðŸ‘‰
